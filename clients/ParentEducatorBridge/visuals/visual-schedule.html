How to Use This Visual Schedule

  • Print and Display: Print this schedule and place it where your child can easily see it (refrigerator, bedroom wall, etc.)
  • Review Together: Each morning, review the schedule with your child to prepare them for the day
  • Check Off Completed: As each activity is completed, check the box or place a velcro dot
  • Individual Cards: Cut out the individual cards (page 2) to create moveable schedule pieces for flexibility
  • Personalize: Add your child's actual photos for even better recognition
  • Laminate: For durability, laminate the schedule and use dry-erase markers or velcro dots
  • Color Coding: Morning (Yellow), School (Blue), Afternoon (Green), Evening (Purple)
