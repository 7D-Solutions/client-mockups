The 3-Second Rule for Following Directions

Tuesday Tips #47 | November 28, 2024

Hi there,

This week I'm sharing the single most powerful technique I've taught parents in 35 years: The 3-Second Rule.

Here's How It Works:

  • Give a simple, one-step direction: "Sit down"
  • Wait 3 seconds in silence (count in your head)
  • If they don't respond, physically guide them to complete it
  • Immediately reward/praise when completed

Why it works: You're teaching that directions WILL be followed‚Äîno negotiation, no repeating. The consistency is what builds the habit.

Try it this week: Pick ONE routine (like sitting for dinner) and use the 3-Second Rule every single time for 7 days. Report back‚ÄîI'd love to hear how it goes!
