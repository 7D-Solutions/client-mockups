## Quick Start Guide

### Step 1: Profile Text
Copy the 2-paragraph profile from `ChristianMingle-Optimized.md` - it's already perfectly sized.

### Step 2: Choose Your Quote
Pick one of the 4 meaningful quotes provided, or use your own favorite Bible verse.

### Step 3: Answer Questions
Select 2-3 of the profile questions to answer. Recommended:
- "What does faith mean to you?"
- "What are you passionate about?"
- "What are you looking for in a partner?"

### Step 4: Prepare Your Photos
You need up to 6 photos:

1. **Main Photo:** Clear headshot (no selfie, professional or natural outdoor shot)
2. **Horseback Riding:** Shows your active lifestyle
3. **Beach/Outdoors:** Enjoying God's creation
4. **Family Photo:** With children/grandkids (wholesome, appropriate)
5. **Church/Mission Trip:** Shows faith in action
6. **Adventure Photo:** Skydiving or another adventurous activity

**Important:**
- No selfies for main photo
- All solo shots preferred (you can have 1-2 group photos max)
- Outdoor photos with natural lighting work best
- Add the suggested captions to each photo

### Step 5: Profile Checklist

Before submitting:
- [ ] Main profile text is 2 paragraphs or less ✓
- [ ] Selected and added meaningful quote
- [ ] Answered 2-3 profile questions
- [ ] Uploaded main headshot photo
- [ ] Uploaded 3-5 additional photos showing lifestyle/hobbies
- [ ] Added captions to all photos
- [ ] Completed all multiple-choice fields
- [ ] Reviewed for typos and clarity
- [ ] Content reflects authentic faith journey
- [ ] Avoided clichés and generic phrases
