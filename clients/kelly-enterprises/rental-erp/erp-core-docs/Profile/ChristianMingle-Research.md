# Christian Mingle Profile Requirements & Best Practices

## Research Date
January 2025

## Profile Photo Requirements

### Photo Limits
- **Up to 6 photos** maximum per profile
- **At least 1 main photo** required (clear headshot)
- All photos must go through approval process (24-48 hours)

### Photo Best Practices
- ✅ Clear solo headshots
- ✅ Outdoor photos in natural light
- ✅ Neutral clothing (face is focal point)
- ✅ Photos from different settings showing hobbies/lifestyle
- ❌ Avoid selfies (especially as main photo)
- ❌ No group shots as main photo
- ❌ Limit selfies to 1-2 max (appear narcissistic)
- ❌ Limit group pics to 1-2 max (appears like hiding something)

**Key Stat**: Profiles with photos are **10x more likely** to attract views

### Photo Descriptions
- Add 1-2 lines to each photo
- Provide insight into your life and personality
- Help matches understand context

## Profile Text Guidelines

### Length Requirements
- **2 paragraphs maximum** recommended
- **3-5 sentences** per paragraph
- Give sense of who you are without giving everything away

### Content Structure
**First Paragraph**: Mention work, hobbies, friends, and/or family

**Second Paragraph**: Convey personality, values, and what you're looking for

### What to Include
- ✅ Faith details (church involvement, spiritual journey)
- ✅ Core values that guide your life
- ✅ Unique differences that make you stand out
- ✅ Specific examples (not generic statements)
- ✅ Authentic voice
- ✅ Honest self-description

### What to Avoid
- ❌ Clichés and generic phrases
- ❌ Sounding shallow or appearance-focused
- ❌ Lengthy descriptions
- ❌ Being vague or mysterious

## Profile Questions
- Christian Mingle includes **6 profile questions**
- You don't have to answer all of them
- **Recommended**: Answer at least 2-3 questions
- Use these to let your voice and personality shine
- Most other profile fields are multiple choice, so written answers help you stand out

## Enhanced Profile Features
- Space for meaningful quote (display prominently)
- Enhanced photo layout
- Space to describe yourself
- Multiple-choice preference fields

## Approval Process
- All profile content (photos and text) goes through approval
- Can take up to **24 hours** for initial approval
- Complete profile thoroughly from the start to avoid delays

## Tips for Success

### Be Specific & Authentic
- Draw attention to what makes you unique
- Think about things you wish more people knew about you
- Use specific examples instead of generic statements
- Be honest about your faith journey

### Faith-Focused Content
- It's okay to admit you haven't been to church in a while but want to go more
- If you're very active in church, mention specific involvement
- Share what your relationship with God means to you
- Mention mission work, service, or ministry experience

### Personality & Voice
- Write like you speak
- Show your personality in 3-5 sentences
- Convey enthusiasm without overdoing it
- Let your authentic self come through

### Profile Optimization Checklist
- [ ] 1-6 high-quality photos uploaded
- [ ] Main photo is clear solo headshot
- [ ] Profile text is 2 paragraphs or less
- [ ] Mentioned work/hobbies/family
- [ ] Included faith details
- [ ] Answered 2-3 profile questions
- [ ] Added meaningful quote
- [ ] Photo descriptions added
- [ ] All content is specific and authentic
- [ ] Avoided clichés and generic phrases
- [ ] Completed all multiple-choice fields

## Key Success Factors
1. **Photos are critical** - 10x more views with photos
2. **Be concise** - 2 paragraphs max keeps attention
3. **Be specific** - Details make you memorable
4. **Show faith authentically** - Don't be generic about beliefs
5. **Answer questions** - Let your voice be heard
6. **Complete profile fully** - Demonstrates serious intent

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Writing too much (information overload)
- Using only selfies or group photos
- Being vague or mysterious
- Using clichés like "I love to laugh" or "looking for my soulmate"
- Not mentioning faith at all
- Sounding negative or listing what you don't want
- Leaving profile incomplete
- Not updating photos regularly
