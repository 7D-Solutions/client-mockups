## Profile Question Answers

### Question: "What does faith mean to you?"
**Answer:**
Faith is the foundation of everything I do. It's not just about Sunday services - it's actively serving my church, going on mission trips, and being God's hands and feet in the world. My relationship with Christ guides my decisions and gives purpose to my adventures, whether I'm spending time with my grandkids or jumping out of airplanes!

### Question: "What are you passionate about?"
**Answer:**
I'm passionate about serving others through mission work, staying active in God's creation (horseback riding, beach days, outdoor adventures), and creating meaningful memories with my 5 children and grandkids. I also love pushing boundaries - there's something about jumping out of a plane that reminds me to trust God completely!

### Question: "What are you looking for in a partner?"
**Answer:**
Someone who shares my deep faith in Christ and understands that a relationship centered on God is the strongest foundation. I'm looking for an active, adventurous spirit who values family as much as I do and is ready to serve together - whether that's on a mission trip, in our local church, or simply living out our faith daily in the countryside.

### Question: "How do you spend your free time?"
**Answer:**
You'll find me riding horses through scenic trails, soaking up sunshine at the beach, or exploring the great outdoors. I love staying physically fit and active. Family time with my kids and grandkids is precious, and I'm passionate about serving through my church and mission trips. And when I really want an adrenaline rush, I jump out of perfectly good airplanes!

### Question: "What makes you unique?"
**Answer:**
I'm a faith-filled grandmother who jumps out of airplanes! I've raised 5 amazing children, served on mission trips around the world, and I'm still looking for new adventures. I live in the countryside, stay fit riding horses, and believe that God wants us to live life to the fullest - with purpose, passion, and a parachute when necessary.

### Question: "Describe a perfect day"
**Answer:**
Starting with morning devotions and coffee on my country porch, then horseback riding through beautiful trails. Spending the afternoon at the beach or serving at church, followed by a family dinner surrounded by my children and grandkids. Ending the day under the stars, grateful for God's blessings and excited for tomorrow's adventures.

## Quick Tips for Your Profile

### Do's:
- ✅ Keep main text to those 2 paragraphs
- ✅ Answer 2-3 profile questions to let your personality shine
- ✅ Add captions to all 6 photos
- ✅ Use your best clear headshot as main photo
- ✅ Include variety of photos showing faith, family, and hobbies
- ✅ Be specific about your faith journey and church involvement

### Don'ts:
- ❌ Don't use selfies as your main photo
- ❌ Don't write more than 2 paragraphs in main description
- ❌ Don't use group photos where you can't be easily identified
- ❌ Don't be vague - specific details make you memorable
- ❌ Don't forget to add a meaningful quote
